What is Hackoverflow?


Hack Overflow is a unique hack-a-thon that aims to combine technical ability with managerial prowess. Skills in technical and management fields are equally important for any start-up to flourish.
Hack Overflow aims to be that platform which brings coders, web developers, entrepreneurs, business analysts and marketers together under one roof and makes them work alongside each other to scale up their start-ups.
Various sessions stressing on the importance of technical and management skills by experts will solidify the participants’ basics and they will get to apply their newly gained knowledge in the pitching round soon after.

About Us



S.H.E.I.L.D (C.S.E.D)

C.S.E.D is an organistaion originating from VIT, Vellore. It is a platform for budding entrepreneurs to scale up their startup from ideation stage. We help the young generation of entrepreneurs understand the ins and outs of the startup world. In addition to this we hold various events and workshops in schools and colleges to help students ideate in early stage. An important part of our work is to connect entrepreneurs to investors and interns through our growing network, providing them the required opportunities to grow.

Venturesity- VIT (Skillenza)

“To help hone quality talent through hackathons” Venturesity is a startup based from Bangalore which focuses on building technical knowledge through projects conducted in their hackathon. Having collaborated with other tech startups and conducting successful hackathons around India, such as Appsaholic, SimpliLearn, and OLA Cabs Appathon, Venturesity hopes to do more. They want to bring the same workspace to colleges, to improve the fundamentals of the future engineers. Apart from hosting events, they have online courses and competitions, called skillenza, which feature quality mentors, to help extend the learning process beyond their events. For more information.
